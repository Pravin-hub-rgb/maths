Subgraph :

Consider a graph G(V,E). A graph g=(v,e) is said to be a subgraph of G if e⊂E & v⊂V such that edges in 'e' are incident only with the vertices in v.

Complement of Graph

The complement of a graph G is defined as a simple graph with the same vertex set as G and where two vertices u and v are adjacent only when they are not adjacent in G.
